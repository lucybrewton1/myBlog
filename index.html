My Favorite Hobby
Writing

I love writing because it makes you in charge of a world. I'm not normally in charge in the real world, but when I'm writing I can decide what I want everyone to do. It's also a really good way to just get lost and lose track of everything. When I need a break from life, I like to write and shape things how I'd like them to be.
